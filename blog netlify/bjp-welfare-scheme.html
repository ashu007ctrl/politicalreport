BJP's Welfare Schemes: Transforming India

Published Date : May 28, 2025

The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) has launched several welfare schemes aimed at improving the lives of Indian citizens, focusing on economic development, social upliftment, and poverty alleviation. Here are some of the most impactful schemes:

1. Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

Launched in 2016, this scheme provides free LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households. It aims to promote clean cooking fuel and reduce indoor pollution.

2. Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

One of the world's largest healthcare programs, PMJAY provides free health coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for economically vulnerable sections.

3. Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

This scheme aims to provide affordable housing for all by 2022, offering financial assistance and subsidies for home construction and purchase.

4. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

A nationwide cleanliness campaign launched in 2014 to improve sanitation, build toilets, and promote hygiene awareness.

5. PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

A direct income support scheme for farmers, providing ₹6,000 annually to eligible farmers in three installments.

6. Make in India

Launched to boost manufacturing and job creation, this initiative encourages both domestic and foreign companies to set up industries in India.

7. Digital India

A program aimed at improving digital infrastructure, increasing internet connectivity, and making India a digitally empowered society.

Conclusion

The BJP government has implemented several schemes targeting different sectors, including healthcare, employment, sanitation, and financial inclusion. These initiatives continue to shape India’s development journey, benefiting millions across the country.
